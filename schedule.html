HOW TO USE THE RATCHABURI SDAC SCHEDULING APP



Overview

The section below is a scheduling app. It is divided into categories as scheduling for services like:


Midweek Prayers (Marked as Midweek),

Sabbath School (Marked as SS),

Divine Service (Marked as Divine),

AY Programs (Marked as AY Service),

and Notices (Marked as Notices)


The Service Categories

On the main screen, you can find the Midweek, Sabbath School, and Divine Worship scheduling categories
along with the Update and Sharing buttons. Just click on one of these sections to switch between service schedules.


On the upper left corner (marked with three horizontal lines), you can find a menu where AY Service and Notices are placed.
You can also find additional information on the said menu.


How To Edit the Scheduled Appointments

First, click on a category/service.

Second, click on the date.

Third, click on "Edit" then start your input.

Fourth click on "Save"

Fifth, click on "UPDATE"


How To Add New Dates for New Appointments

First, click on a category/service.

Second, go to the bottom and click "New".

Third, start filling in the Date and other info.

Fourth click on "Save"

Fifth, click on "UPDATE"


Screen Too Small For Viewing

If you are using a mobile device, you might find yourself having difficulty viewing or editing the schedule,
you can open the app in a new browser by clicking on THIS LINK


Installing The Ratchaburi SDAC Scheduling App On A Mobile Device

You can install the Ratchaburi SDAC Scheduling app on your iOS or Android mobile for a
streamlined experience by following these steps:


First, click on this Link


Second, click on "Install"


Third, it will prompt you to install an App called AppSheet
from the AppStore or Playstore. Click "GO"


Fourth, Open the newly installed AppSheet.


Fifth, Login with any of your Google Accounts.


Sixth, Allow AppSheet to have access


Seventh, wait for the Ratchaburi SDAC App to load
